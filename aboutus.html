About us

Egyptian entrepreneur and fashion influencer Rawan Alaa launches her inclusive dress line COCO's Fahion Label. After years spent observing the growing fashion gap in the dress market between veiled and unveiled women, with there even being separate stores and designs, COCO has innovated an empowering DRESS line for all women. Having the Arab woman at the heart of the brand, it was an alien notion for Rawan that veiled and unveiled women are separated by their dress styles. COCO's dresses are made to provide the UV-protection, quick-dry and luxury that we all seek when going on any occasion.
